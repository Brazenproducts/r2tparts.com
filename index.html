What Is the Rivian R2T?

The Rivian R2T is the compact truck variant of Rivian's upcoming R2 platform — a smaller, more affordable pickup designed to complement the flagship R1T. The Rivian R2 truck is built for buyers who want genuine off-road capability and electric utility in a mid-size package.

With Rivian's proven electric drivetrain and adventure-focused engineering scaled down to a compact form, the Rivian R2T is expected to target the mid-size truck segment dominated by ICE vehicles like the Tacoma and Ridgeline. The Rivian R2 truck brings Rivian's signature Gear Tunnel-style storage and quad-motor capability rumors to a new price bracket.
